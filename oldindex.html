About SURTIES

Surties started out as a local community for local people of Surat to involve and interact with local happenings of the city and celebrate the true spirit of the city…Khawany Peewanu Majjhani Life. Powered by Surties Media Pvt. Ltd., Surties was founded seven years ago and now has grown to be the most loved community of Surat.

With a goal of serving the people of Surat with true and factual news and happenings of our beloved Surat city – whether it is related to local government bodies, current affairs of the city and state or noteworthy civil individuals, political figures, entertainment, sports and important event of the cities. We filter between actual and fake information circulating on various social media platforms and make sure only authentic news reaches our Followers and Subscribers, so that every Surti is well informed and aware of the current happenings!
